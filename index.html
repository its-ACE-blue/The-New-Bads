Who We Are

The New Bads is an upcoming boyband that is set to revolutionize the music industry with their unique sound and dynamic performances. Comprising three incredibly talented members - Rehaan Gazi (Lead Vocalist), Thanush Hegde (Guitarist), Aman Gupta (Drummer), Gauresh Kurade (Pianist), and Sujal Toppo (Lead Dancer) - The New Bads are here to make a mark.
